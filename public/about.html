About Me

Background

Hello there. My name is Louisa Scott. I was born in Broughty Ferry, Dundee and moved to Sheffield with my family when I was 11. I studied Primary Teaching at Sheffield Hallam University and worked as a teacher and a HLTA until July 2023. I started a new job at FindAUniversity the same month and I am really enjoying the change. I am currently studying a Udemy Course to become a full stack web developer which is exciting. Hopefully this will lead to an exciting new career.
